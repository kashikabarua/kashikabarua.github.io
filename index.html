Enhancing Australia's Aged Care Workforce: Reviewing and Improving Migration Pathways

Policy brief · FORE Australia · Author
Aged careMigration policyWorkforce

Australia faces a projected shortfall of 110,000 direct care workers by 2030. This brief examines the Aged Care Industry Labour Agreement and the PALM scheme's aged care stream, and advises the Department of Health and Aged Care on rights-based migration reforms to strengthen recruitment, retention and worker protections.
